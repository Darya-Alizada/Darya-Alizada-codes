Currency control in practice

How to get money for services from foreign clients

I am a programmer. I develop websites. In November 2018, I was approached by a German startup. We discussed the terms and agreed that they would transfer the payment in dollars to me on check. Before that, I had never worked with foreign exchange.

What is currency control

and Foreign Exchange control. From buying currency in an exchange office to making settlements with foreign companies – all this foreign exchange transactions must be accounted for by the state.

When a payment comes from abroad, the Government and the Central Bank must be sure that the money obtained legally. They ask the recipient for documents that will confirm that he received payment for a legal product or service.

To accept payments in foreign currency, you need to open a foreign currency account. The bank serves foreign currency accounts free of charge.
